DevFount is a human resource firm focused on capacity building, recruitment and HR outsourcing.

The company provides premier services in the selection and placement of entry level, middle management and senior management staff and in the design of focused training programmes that build staff competencies. The company also provides strategic services such as the development of HR strategy, HR policies and processes.

Mission

To partner with clients to understand their needs and deliver customized services and to design and implement focused African Development Projects.

Vision

To support clients to infuse world class HR practices that ensure/engender business profitability and to impact the lives of Africans in the process.

Values

The conduct of our business is guides by the following principles :

  • Creativity
  • Excellence
  • Innovation
  • Integrity
  • Humanity
